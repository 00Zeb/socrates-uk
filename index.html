SoCraTes UK 2013

International Software Craftsmanship open space retreat

September 19th - 22nd, Farncombe estate

What is SoCraTes?

A retreat for 70 open minded individuals who strive to improve their craft and the software industry as a whole.

Set as a self-organized open space over 2 days, expect highly interesting talks, practical sessions and discussions, for developers by developers.

It will run from the evening of Thursday 19th of September until the morning of Sunday 22nd.

Where is it taking place?

The retreat will be hosted at Farncombe, a 300+ acre conference and hotel estate in the beautiful Cotswolds.

All attendee's are expected to stay in the hotel over the 3 days and nights, and we can only accept as many as there are rooms available.

More information regarding accommodation can be found here.

Who should attend?

Anyone who is passionate about software, aware or involved in Software Craftsmanship, and who wants to share their knowledge, experience, expertise, but is also willing and keen to learn from others.

Who's organizing?

SoCraTes UK is organized by the people behind the London Software Craftsmanship Community.

Sounds great! Count me in?

We will open registration soon. To keep up to date with news follow us on twitter.

Inspiration

SoCraTes UK is heavily based on SoCraTes Germany.

After attending the 2012 event, we decided we wanted to bring the same format to the UK.
